"Overview"

   As the economy continues to change, so is the need for people who can easily adapt to these changes and effectively lead an organization where they work. A course in business administration provides students with a goal-focused curriculum and training required to excel in the fields of business, industry and government. Most business administration degrees teaches students to be critical thinkers and innovative leaders by placing special emphasis on core areas like finance, marketing, economics, accounting, statistics, problem solving, human resources and decision-making. A career in Business Administration demands an enterprising attitude and the ability to thrive in stressful situations, collaborate with others and effectively delegate with authority.

   Business administration includes a great deal of problem solving and number crunching, so proficiency in math is always an asset. The University of Eastern Pangasinan offers Bachelor Degree in Business with a general knowledge base and students may specialize in a particular area of their choice. These programs introduce students to a wide range of principles ranging from politics, ethics, sensitivity, innovativeness, creativity and other dynamics enabling them to acquire a deeper appreciation of their interrelationships and their individual impact to an organization. Students may decide which areas of specialization to pursue based on their strengths and interest.

   At the end of the day, the main point of a Business Administration course is not just to produce an efficient business person, but an effective one as well.
In UEP, the BSBA is offered with a business administration major and concentrations in both Financial Management and Marketing Management
